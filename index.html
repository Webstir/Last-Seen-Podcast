Last Seen in the Twilight Zone - True Crime Podcast

Last Seen in the Twilight Zone is a compelling true crime podcast that investigates cold cases, missing persons, and unsolved mysteries in the Binghamton, New York area. Hosted by Quinn and Allie, this podcast shares real stories about real people who have gone missing or been victims of unsolved crimes in what they call "the Twilight Zone."

Each episode of this true crime podcast delves deep into local mysteries, cold case investigations, and disappearances that have left families searching for answers. The hosts bring their expertise in victim advocacy and criminal justice to shed light on cases that deserve attention and justice.

Listen to Last Seen in the Twilight Zone on Apple Podcasts, Spotify, and Audible. The podcast covers a range of true crime topics including murder mysteries, unsolved disappearances, cold cases, and criminal investigations in the upstate New York region.

True Crime Podcast Episodes

Explore episodes covering cases like the disappearance of Michele Harris, cold cases from the Binghamton area, unsolved murders, and missing persons investigations. Each episode is carefully researched and presented with respect for the victims and their families.

About the Hosts

Quinn is a victim advocate based in Binghamton, NY with over a decade of experience working with victims of crime. Allie brings her background in criminal justice and personal experience with violent crime to the podcast. Together, they investigate and share these important true crime stories.
